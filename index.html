Est. 1962 · Madurai

Where Every Sweet Holds a Memory

In 1962, Nagora Krishnaswamy began crafting sweets from a small kitchen in Madurai's old town. He believed every mithai should carry a story — the warmth of a festival, the joy of a wedding, the love of a grandmother's hands.

Today, three generations later, we still follow his original recipes. Our ghee is sourced from the same farms in Palani, our sugar from Thiruvanamalai, and our passion from every family that has trusted Nagora Sweets to sweeten their most precious moments.
